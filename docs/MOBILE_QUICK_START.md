## Troubleshooting

**Panel won't move?**
- Try swiping harder
- Or just tap the header

**Layout looks wrong?**
- Reload page (pull down to refresh)
- Check browser zoom (should be 100%)

**Can't see controls?**
- Swipe up to expand
- They're hidden when collapsed
